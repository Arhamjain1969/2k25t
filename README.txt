TWO SEPARATE SYSTEMS:
=====================

STUDENT INVITATIONS (Grade 12):
- Open: index.html
- For Grade 12 students
- Simple form with Name, Grade (12th), Section

TEACHER INVITATIONS (From Grade 11):
- Open: teacher-index.html  
- For teachers invited by Grade 11 students
- Form with Teacher Name and Subject only
- No designation/admin fields
